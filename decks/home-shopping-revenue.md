## Slide 6 — Grid168 for Home Shopping Projections

Grid168 uses underlying viewership metrics of a home shopping network to project hourly rates and sales projections for shopping networks and programs distributed by ConsumerEXP.

**Outputs:**
- Hourly revenue forecast
- Projected sales by schedule block
- Rate card support
- Sales potential by time period
- TV listings from Grid168 schedule data
