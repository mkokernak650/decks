## Slide 8 — Grid168

Grid168 uses underlying viewership metrics of a home shopping network to project hourly rates and sales projections for shopping networks and programs distributed by ConsumerEXP.

**Core value:** Turn programming schedules and viewership assumptions into projected sales and hourly rate guidance.
