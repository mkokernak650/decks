## Slide 2 — Distribution Challenge

Networks need more than carriage conversations. They need a complete path from distribution planning to revenue tracking.

**Challenges:**
- Fragmented distribution outlets
- Different economics by platform
- Limited visibility into performance
- Difficulty proving revenue value by market
- Need for EPG, listings, schedules, reporting, and sales attribution

---

## Slide 3 — ConsumerEXP Distribution Approach

ConsumerEXP helps connect content, platform opportunities, revenue models, and measurable performance.

**Process:**
1. Review network/programming assets
2. Identify target distribution outlets
3. Build the revenue model
4. Support rate card and sales structure
5. Track calls, sales, markets, and KPIs
6. Report performance to partners
