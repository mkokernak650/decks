## Slide 3 — The Market Problem

Advertisers and networks need more than placement. They need a complete way to understand which markets, households, demographics, time periods, and calls are producing revenue.

**Pain points:**
- Fragmented TV inventory
- Hard-to-measure campaign performance
- Weak visibility into call and order conversion
- Limited demographic insight for ad sales and distribution
- Unclear ROI across cash buy and revenue-share deals

## Slide 7 — Grid168: Hourly Revenue Forecasting

Grid168 uses underlying viewership metrics of a home shopping network or program to project hourly rates and sales projections.

**Key uses:**
- Forecast hourly revenue
- Support pricing decisions
- Build ad sales rate cards
- Estimate home shopping sales by hour
- Create TV listings from Grid168 scheduling data
